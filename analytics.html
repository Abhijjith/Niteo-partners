Pharmaceutical industry is obsessed with data. With the rise in sensing technologies and digital services, this data pile is only going to increase (almost 4 times) in the next decade. However, if you look at data-to-insight developed rate, the picture becomes very bleak. The industry has a poor track record of creating solutions from massive mountains of data.

With outcomes-based care taking centre stage in major healthcare systems, there is no turning back when it comes to data. We help pharmaceutical companies in creating a data-driven decision making machinery by converting “raw data” into “smart data”, and then channelling the “smart data” to generate insights about key markets, competition, pricing and reimbursement, and physician and payer behaviour in different therapeutic areas.

Our customised analytics offering allows pharmaceutical companies to create unique data-views to answer complicated questions such as:

  • What is the time-to-market for existing drugs and therapies?
  • How can I design the sales incentive plans for different territories?
  • Which channels offer the highest ROI?

Our solutions include:

  • Data Management and Data Visualisation.
  • Data Warehousing and Data Mapping.
  • Commercial Analytics and Predictive Reporting.
