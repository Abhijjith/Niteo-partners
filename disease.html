In today’s value and outcomes based assessment environment, product differentiation has become extremely critical for getting payer buy-in. The competitive positioning of a particular product has to be unique not only in terms of efficacy, safety, convenience and price but also along the cost-effectiveness dimension.

At Niteo, market access driven competitive intelligence lies at the heart of the way we formulate a good market access strategy.

We support our clients develop an effective market access strategy by providing focused competitive research services so that the existing offering can be compared across the care continuum:

  • Competition Trackers and Alerts (using real-time tracking algorithms)
  • Disease and Therapy Area Assessment
  • Pricing and Reimbursement Newsletters (with customised competitive insights)





CASE STUDY

Background: A top pharmaceutical company approached us to develop competition-tracking dashboard with 25 different variables for their top 10 competitors. They were planning to launch a new product and wanted to ensure that real-time competitive insights are captured in strategic planning.

Solution: We developed the competition tracker using semantic search techniques to collect information and quantitative ML scoring techniques to classify the information retrieved on 25 different variables. This was then delivered through a weekly newsletter format to senior leaders of the team.

Our findings informed the strategic planning teams with on-going competitive activities in the market place.
